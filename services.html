Premium Terry Towels

High-quality, 100% cotton towels available in a wide range of sizes, colors, and GSM. Perfect for personal, hotel, and commercial use with customization options.

Authentic Indian Spices

Exporting pure and natural Indian spices including turmeric, cumin, black pepper, and more, sourced directly from trusted farmers to ensure taste, aroma, and quality.

Multani Mitti (Fuller’s Earth)

Supplying pure, chemical-free Multani Mitti in powder and lump forms, widely used in cosmetics, Ayurvedic remedies, and skincare treatments globally.

Eco-friendly products such as dried cow dung cakes, powders, and incense, used for religious rituals, organic farming, and sustainable energy solutions.

Kolhapuri Chappals

Handcrafted leather sandals from Kolhapur, known for their durability, elegance, and comfort—blending traditional craftsmanship with modern fashion appeal.
