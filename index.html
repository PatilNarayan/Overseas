    About

    About Us

    Amritva Overseas is a trusted and growing export-import firm based in India, specializing in the global trade of high-quality Indian products. We are committed to delivering excellence, sustainability, and authenticity in every consignment, serving clients across the globe with integrity and professionalism.

    • Premium quality products meeting international standards.
    • Transparent dealings, timely shipments, and customized solutions.
    • Empowering local artisans and promoting eco-friendly trade.

    Our vision is to become a globally recognized and respected name in the export-import industry by promoting traditional Indian products and ensuring customer satisfaction through reliable service. We offer a wide range of products including Terry Towels, Indian Spices, Multani Mitti, Cow Dung Products, Handicrafts, and Kolhapuri Chappals—each crafted with care, sustainability, and attention to detail.

    Read More

    Premium Terry Towels

    We supply high-quality, soft, and durable terry towels made from 100% cotton, ideal for personal, commercial, and hospitality use. Custom sizes, colors, and GSM ranges are available as per client requirements.

    Authentic Indian Spices

    We export a wide range of natural and aromatic Indian spices including turmeric, cumin, coriander, black pepper, and more—sourced directly from trusted farmers to ensure purity, flavor, and freshness.

    Multani Mitti (Fuller’s Earth)

    We offer pure, chemical-free Multani Mitti in both powder and lump forms. Rich in minerals, it is widely used in skincare and cosmetic applications to detoxify skin and enhance beauty naturally.

    Eco-Friendly Cow Dung Products

    We supply dried cow dung cakes, powders, and incense sticks used for religious rituals, organic farming, and eco-friendly fuel. All products are hygienically processed and safely packaged for global supply.

    Handcrafted Indian Art

    We promote Indian artisans by exporting beautiful handcrafted items including wooden crafts, decorative pieces, and festival gifts. Every item reflects India’s rich culture, tradition, and skilled craftsmanship.

    Kolhapuri Chappals

    We bring India’s iconic handcrafted leather Kolhapuri chappals to the world. Known for their durability, comfort, and traditional design, these chappals are perfect for ethnic and modern fashion lovers alike.
